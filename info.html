The planets

Each planet plays an important role in our character and personality, our tastes and our way of being.


Sun

The Sun speaks of the real core of the person, the inner self, the characteristics of central importance. It also shows us the vitality and the ability to assert oneself, describes the general tonality of the being that colors everything else.


Equivalent sign: Leo


Moon

The Moon represents our feelings and emotions, receptivity, imagination and the basic tonality of the feelings of the person. It also has an effect on the sense of rhythm, timing and opportunity, influences our adaptability to change, our mobility and versatility.


Equivalent sign: Cancer


Mercury

Mercury represents reason, common sense, how we rationalize something. It symbolizes the spoken and written word, in the way of putting order, thinking and evaluating, the learning process and skills.


Equivalent signs: Gemini and Virgo


Venus

Venus gives us the sense of beauty, enjoyment of pleasure, conscious aesthetics, love of harmony, sociability, obtaining pleasure in relationships and eroticism.

Equivalent signs: Libra and Taurus


Mars

Mars represents the energy and instinct of a person, their courage, determination, the freedom of spontaneous impulse. It also describes readiness to action, the way one undertakes things as well as simple aggression.


Equivalent sign: Aries


Jupiter

The search for individual purpose and meaning, optimism, hope and a sense of justice are represented by Jupiter. Also faith, the basic philosophy of life, the effort to grow spiritually and expansion.


Equivalent sign: Sagittarius


Saturn

Saturn shows how we experience "reality," where we find resistance and discover our limitations. It represents the conscious conviction and moral, the laws and rules we choose to obey. It also speaks of our power to tolerate and the ability to concentrate, gives qualities such as honesty, caution and reserve.


Equivalent sign: Capricorn


Uranus

Uranus symbolizes intuition, conveys sudden inspiration and instant understanding. The openness to everything new, unknown and unusual. It is also associated with a kind of obstinate rebellion. It is said to be representative of astrology.


Equivalent sign: Aquarius


Neptune

This planet bestows the suprasensory, opens the doors to mystical experience and the transcendental. At this level it is difficult to discern where perception is confused with deception, the illusion and false appearances, and therefore Neptune is associated with all these, with drugs and all kinds of pseudo-realities.


Equivalent sign: Pisces


Pluto

Pluto describes how we handle power, personal and non-personal, either suffering the power of others or exercising it ourselves. It describes how we go to meet the demonic and magic, our power of regeneration and our ability of radical change and rebirth: the cycles of death and transformation.


Equivalent sign: Scorpio

The 4 elements

The four elements can be considered as four basic principles of life. The emphasis or lack of emphasis of these elements in the individual horoscope reveals fundamental aspects of personality.


Fire

People with a strong fire emphasis are spontaneous and impulsive, they apply their energy with all their heart. Their emotional response is quick and have a lively imagination.


Fire signs: Aries, Leo and Sagittarius.



Air

Air people are quick and lively. They apply their energy in very different ways. They tend to intellectualize their feelings and expectations.


Air signs: Libra, Aquarius and Gemini.



Water

People with a strong water emphasis are the sentimental type. They are very sensitive. Their imaginative and emotional lives are rich and deep.


Water signs: Cancer, Scorpio and Pisces.



Earth

Earth people react slowly and calmly. Their dedication is constant and persevering. Emotionally, they are deeply rooted and are slow to change.


Earth signs: Taurus, Virgo and Capricorn.
