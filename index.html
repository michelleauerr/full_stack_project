Welcome to Astrology !


Since the beginning of times, human beings have observed the sky and differentiated between two apparently similar elements: the fixed stars, which remained immobile with regards to the others, and the wandering stars, (in ancient Greek: Asteres planetai), which seemed to change their position throughout the year.

The latter would go on to constitute the astrological planets in ancient times, which continue to be used to this day. Although the modern astrologer does not work with gods or quasi-divine characters, they are aware that the universe is a continuum of matter, energy and vibrations of different frequencies, which manifests itself in many forms and at many levels and that they are all interconnected.

If you want to learn about this world and delve a little into it, browse through the images to see further.
